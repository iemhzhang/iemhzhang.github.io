Biography

Zhang Menghua is a member of the School of Energy and Power Engineering, Huazhong University of Science and Technology, majoring in refrigeration and cryogenic engineering. Her research interests include air conditioning system fault diagnosis and building energy consumption prediction based on data mining and deep learning algorithms. She was responsible for the optimization project of the cold and heat source control system of the SAIC Volkswagen Energy Center based on data mining and deep learning algorithms, and participated in the National Natural Science Foundation of China project “Research on Refrigeration and Air Conditioning Fault Diagnosis Methods for Pattern Identification and Integrated Learning under the Big Data Framework”
