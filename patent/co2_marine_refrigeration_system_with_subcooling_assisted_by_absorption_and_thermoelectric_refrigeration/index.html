CO2 Marine Refrigeration System With Subcooling Assisted By Absorption and Thermoelectric Refrigeration

Oct 1, 2017

The invention discloses a CO2 marine refrigeration system with absorption and thermoelectric refrigeration in cooperation with auxiliary subcooling. The solution outlet of the absorber of the present invention is connected to the solution inlet of the generator through a solution pump, the refrigerant vapor outlet of the generator is connected to the second refrigerant inlet of the evaporator through a condenser and a pressure reducing valve, and the second refrigerant outlet of the evaporator is connected to the absorber; The absorber is connected through a throttle valve; the compressor outlet, gas cooler, thermoelectric subcooler, evaporator two, expander and evaporator one inlet are connected in sequence, and the evaporator one outlet is connected to the compressor inlet; the power manager is connected to the expansion separately Machine and thermoelectric supercooler. The invention can cool the CO2 at the outlet of the gas cooler by recycling the waste heat and pressure, is easy to realize, and can reduce its temperature below the ambient temperature, and can significantly reduce the irreversible loss in the high-pressure CO2 throttling process
